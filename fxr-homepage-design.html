FXR Racing website Design

THE CLIENT

THE MISSION
The company's line is updated four times a year and they offer different deals and sale events on a monthly basis. As a part of the design team I was responsible for the imaginary on the website, including updating product pages and creating banners for the home page.

MY ROLE
Branding, Identity, Web Design, UX / UI, Studio Photography, Photo Editing
